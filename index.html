TV Activation Instructions

Your step-by-step guide to activate your TV and OTT platforms in the USA.

TV Activation Steps

  1. Turn on your TV and ensure it is connected to the internet.
  2. Navigate to the settings menu and select "Activate Device."
  3. Write down the activation code displayed on your screen.
  4. Visit the activation website on your computer or smartphone.
  5. Enter the activation code and follow the on-screen instructions.
  6. Once completed, your TV will be activated and ready to use.

OTT Platform Activation

Netflix

  1. Open the Netflix app on your TV.
  2. Select "Sign In" and choose "Activate Device."
  3. Visit the activation page on another device.
  4. Enter the activation code displayed on your TV.
  5. Sign in to your Netflix account to complete activation.

Hulu

  1. Launch the Hulu app on your TV.
  2. Select "Log In" and note the activation code.
  3. Go to the activation page on another device.
  4. Enter the activation code and log in to your Hulu account.

Amazon Prime Video

  1. Open the Prime Video app on your TV.
  2. Select "Register on Amazon Website."
  3. Visit the activation page on another device.
  4. Enter the activation code and sign in to your Amazon account.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if my activation code doesn't work?

Ensure you entered the code correctly. If the issue persists, restart your TV and try again.

Can I activate multiple devices?

Yes, but some platforms may limit the number of devices per account.

Do I need a subscription to activate?

Yes, you need an active subscription to the respective OTT platform.
